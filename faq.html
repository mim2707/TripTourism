Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Do I need a visa to visit Uzbekistan?

Yes, you will need to submit electronic visa application before coming to Uzbekistan. To get more information, please visit official website of Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

Do I need PCR test to enter to Uzbekistan?

Regarding the government policies about COVID-19, if you have fully vaccination certificate, you are not required to provide PCR test after 1st of April.

What is the cost of underground in Tashkent?

The cost of public transportation is similar for all types. It costs 1.400 soums which is equal 0.095 GBP.

Average cost of eating in restaurants?

It depends on your expectations. But the average cost is approximately 50.000 soums.

What kind of documents do I need to provide when I come to the hotel for check-in?

You will not need so many documents. It is enough to provide your passport.
